# Client Portal User Guide

Welcome to your Wavelaunch Client Portal! This guide will help you navigate your portal and get the most out of your experience.

## Getting Started

### Receiving Your Invite

You'll receive an email with an invite link from your Wavelaunch representative. The link looks like:
```
https://wavelaunch.com/portal/invite/abc123...
```

**Important Notes**:
- The link expires after 7 days
- It can only be used once
- Keep it secure - don't share it with anyone

### Creating Your Account

1. Click the invite link in your email
2. You'll see a welcome page with your agency information
3. Create a strong password:
   - At least 8 characters
   - Include uppercase and lowercase letters
   - Include at least one number
   - Include at least one special character (!@#$%^&*)
   - Example: `MySecure123!`
4. Confirm your password
5. Click "Complete Registration"

You're now ready to use your portal!

## Logging In

### First Time Login
After creating your account, you'll be automatically logged in.

### Future Logins
1. Go to: `https://wavelaunch.com/portal/login`
2. Enter your email address
3. Enter your password
4. Click "Sign In"

### Forgot Your Password?
1. Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Click the link and create a new password

## The Onboarding Questionnaire

The first time you log in, you'll see the onboarding wizard. This helps us create your personalized business plan.

### 6-Step Process

#### Step 1: Business Basics (Required)
Tell us about your business:
- What industry or niche are you in?
- What's your vision for your business?
- What are your main goals?

#### Step 2: Target Audience (Required)
Help us understand who you serve:
- Which industry do you target?
- Who is your ideal client or customer?
- What age group are they?
- What are their demographics?

#### Step 3: Value Proposition (Required)
Explain what makes you unique:
- What problems do your clients face?
- How do you solve these problems?
- What makes you different from competitors?

#### Step 4: Brand Identity (Required)
Share your brand vision:
- How do you want people to see your brand?
- What personality does your brand have?
- What fonts and styles do you prefer?
- What emotions should your brand evoke?

#### Step 5: Growth & Vision (Optional)
Share your growth plans:
- How do you plan to scale?
- What marketing channels do you use?
- What are your growth goals?
- Any specific deadlines or milestones?

#### Step 6: Your Story (Optional)
Tell us about your journey:
- Key professional milestones
- What inspired you to start this business?
- Your social media handles
- Any additional information

### Completing the Questionnaire

- Fill out all required fields (marked with *)
- Optional fields help us create a better plan, but you can skip them
- Click "Previous" to go back and edit answers
- Click "Next" to move forward
- Click "Complete Onboarding" on the final step

Once submitted, our team will review your information and create your business plan!

## Documents Library

Access all your files in one place.

### Viewing Documents

1. Click "Documents" in the navigation menu
2. You'll see two tabs:
   - **Business Plans**: Your strategic documents
   - **Deliverables**: Monthly program deliverables

### Filtering Documents

Use the dropdown menus to filter:
- **Type**: Business Plans, Deliverables, or All
- **Status**: Draft, In Progress, Delivered, or All
- **Sort by**: Date, Month, or Version

### Downloading Documents

1. Find the document you want
2. Click the download icon or "Download" button
3. The PDF will download to your computer

### Document Status

Documents can have different statuses:
- **Draft**: Work in progress, may change
- **In Progress**: Currently being worked on
- **Delivered**: Complete and ready for you
- **Approved**: You've approved the document

## Messaging

Communicate securely with your Wavelaunch team.

### Starting a Conversation

1. Click "Messages" in the navigation
2. Click "New Message"
3. Enter a subject line
4. Write your message
5. (Optional) Attach a file
6. Click "Send"

### Viewing Messages

1. Go to "Messages"
2. See all your conversation threads
3. Click any thread to read messages
4. Unread messages show a blue dot

### Replying to Messages

1. Open a conversation thread
2. Type your reply in the box at the bottom
3. Click "Send"

Messages are delivered instantly to your team!

### Uploading Files

When sending a message, you can attach:
- Images (JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP)
- Documents (PDF, Word, Excel)
- Text files (TXT, CSV)

Maximum file size: 10MB

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

**Problem**: "Invalid credentials" error
**Solution**:
1. Double-check your email and password
2. Use "Forgot Password?" if needed
3. Contact your team for help

**Problem**: "Account not active"
**Solution**: Your account may not be activated yet. Contact your team.

### Invite Link Expired

**Problem**: "Invite link has expired"
**Solution**: Contact your Wavelaunch representative for a new invite link.

### Can't Download Documents

**Problem**: Download button doesn't work
**Solution**:
1. Try a different browser
2. Disable browser extensions temporarily
3. Check your download folder - it may have downloaded
4. Contact your team for direct file access

### Not Receiving Notifications

**Problem**: Missing notification emails
**Solution**:
1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Add wavelaunch.com to your safe senders list
3. Check your notification preferences in Settings
4. Verify your email address is correct

### File Upload Fails

**Problem**: "File too large" or upload error
**Solution**:
1. Check file size (must be under 10MB)
2. Check file type (see allowed types above)
3. Try compressing large files
4. Contact your team for alternative upload methods
